Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Technique is a method of time management that uses a timer to break down work into intervals (Pomodoros). Each Pomodoro is separated by a short break until four Pomodoros have been completed. After the fourth Pomodoro, a longer break is taken before returning to work.
